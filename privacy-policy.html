Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you visit https://ireland.thejacksons.live/ (the “Website”) or interact with our services, such as signing up for early access to tickets for The Jacksons live show at 3Arena, Dublin on 15 November 2026. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

The data controller responsible for your personal data is Global Tour Creatives (Ireland) Limited (referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”).

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@globaltourcreatives.com

Postal Address: 2/3 Castle Street, Bray, Co Wicklow, Ireland

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect only the personal data that is necessary for the purposes described below. On this Website, we primarily collect:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address when you sign up for early access to tickets or presale notifications.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent) collected automatically via cookies or similar technologies.
  • Marketing Preferences: Your consent choices for receiving communications.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (special categories under GDPR) unless required for the event and with your explicit consent.

3. How We Collect Your Personal Data

  • Directly from you: When you complete the signup form on the Website.
  • Automatically: Through cookies, server logs, or analytics tools (see section on Cookies below).
  • From third parties: Potentially from ticket platforms if you purchase tickets.

4. Legal Basis for Processing Your Personal Data (GDPR Article 6)

We process your personal data on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given clear consent for us to send you marketing communications or ticket presale information (you can withdraw this at any time).
  • Contract: To provide the service you requested (e.g., adding you to the presale list and notifying you about tickets).
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our Website, understand audience interest in the event, and communicate effectively about the show (balanced against your rights).
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with laws, such as record-keeping or responding to lawful requests.

5. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your data for:

  • Sending you presale access details, ticket information, and updates about The Jacksons Ireland show.
  • Managing your signup and preferences.
  • Improving the Website and our services (analytics).
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Preventing fraud or misuse.

We will not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or significant effects on you.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Website may use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., for analytics or essential functionality). These help us provide a better user experience and understand traffic.

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the Website to function.
  • Analytics Cookies: To measure visitor numbers and behaviour (anonymised where possible).

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more details, we may provide a separate Cookie Policy or banner on the site.

7. Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share your data with trusted third parties, including:

  • Email service providers such as Mailchimp (or other artist email services) to manage and send communications.
  • Ticket platforms and event partners, specifically Ticketmaster, 3Arena, The Jacksons, and Sister Sledge (where necessary for presale access, ticket sales, or event delivery).
  • Other service providers (e.g., website hosts or analytics providers) who process data on our behalf under GDPR-compliant agreements.

We do not sell your personal data. Any international transfers outside the EEA will use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures (e.g., encryption, access controls) to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. For example:

  • Signup data for presale: Retained until after the event or until you unsubscribe.
  • Technical logs: Shorter retention periods.

10. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights (subject to certain conditions):

  • Access , Rectification, Erasure, Restriction, Objection, Portability, and the right to Withdraw Consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (www.dataprotection.ie) or your local supervisory authority.

11. Children’s Data

Our Website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to us at the contact details provided in Section 1.
